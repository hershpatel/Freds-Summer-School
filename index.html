Fred Zheng

I am currently a junior at East Brunswick High School. I enjoy playing Tennis, folding Origami and like any other boy of my age, playing video games. My favorite subjects are science and math. I have won numerous awards for science including first place in state in Science Olympiad, 8th place in state for Chemistry League, and 6th place in state for Merck State Science Day competition. I hope to spread the joy of learning science to other people, forever preserving the curiosity of our inner child.

Justin May

I am currently a rising Junior at EBHS. I am apart of the school's swim team, a club swim team, varsity trac k, Model United Nations, Science Olympiad, Academic Team, ACLU, Amnesty International, FBLA, and many other activities. Jason Seibel

I have a passion for many things, but in general STEM and economics are my favorite subjects. Outside of school I play percussion, guitar, piano, and am dabbling with trumpet. Additionally, I have placed first in the state for Science Olympiad and I have participated in Biology League and the TEAMS competition. Currently I am a sophomore and next year I will be taking AP chemistry along with physics honors. This year I have been trying to restart the Robotics Team and hopefully the Board of Education will make it a club for next school year. Robotics Team is a perfect fit for me because it combines engineering, computer science, and electronics into one project. Overall, I take great joy in explaining concepts to friends and I love to find new metaphors and descriptions that will communicate a concept better than teachers normally do.
